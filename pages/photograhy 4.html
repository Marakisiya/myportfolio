PHOTOGRAPHY

Photography and Advertisement Campaign

This course assignment involves the creation of an advertisement for a fictional client. The assignment involved the use of photography as the basis for an advertisement campaign for a product. Thus, for the assignment I decided to create an advertisement for a tailoring services that produce traditional Hausa children's clothes. The advertisement will showcase a typical embroidery work on a given plain cloth material with a matching head cap. This type of clothing are worn by kids during festival in mostly Hausa society in West Africa. The image on the right is a sample of the design I completed for the advertisement campaign. While the designing the advertisement, I conceptualize and plan a creative photo shoot and produce the final advertisement for the chosen product. I ensure that the following are included in the design:
• I self-produced all the photographs used in the advertisement. No pre-produced backgrounds or images may be used.
• I ensured that the photograph incorporates model photography in combination with landscape in a visually captivating way.
• I allowed the photograph to do the talking by keeping the layout basic.
